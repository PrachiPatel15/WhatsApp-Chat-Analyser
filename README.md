## WhatsApp Chat Analyser
### A streamlit app to analyse your WhatsApp Chats

__Demo Link:__ https://wca-prachi.herokuapp.com/

### Aim:
* To analyse the statistics of any group chat or any individual chats

### This App includes:
* Total Messages
* Total Words
* Total Media Shared
* Total Link Shared
* Monthly Timeline
* Weekly Timeline
* Activity Map
** Most Busy Day
** Most Busy Month
* Weekly Activity Map
* Most Busy User
* Most COmmon Words
* Emoji Analysis
